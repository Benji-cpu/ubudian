# Monetisation comps — wellness aggregators & spiritual subscriptions

- Date: 2026-05-07
- Researcher: claude (background agent)

## TL;DR

- The wellness/spiritual recurring-access market clusters into three price bands: **$5–10/mo for content-only** (apps, newsletters), **$15–25/mo for content + live community** (Sounds True One, Calm), and **$70–180/mo for in-person studio access** (ClassPass low/mid tiers, Yoga Barn unlimited).
- The dominant ladder is **Free (loss-leader library) → ~$5–6/mo annual / ~$10/mo monthly (premium content) → premium tier only for live/in-person access**. Insight Timer (free → $5/mo annual) and Calm (free → $5.83/mo annual) are the canonical templates.
- **Annual prepay carries a ~40–50% discount over monthly** across nearly every comp (Insight Timer $60/yr vs $9.99/mo; Calm $79.99/yr vs $16.99/mo). This is the strongest pricing convention in the category.
- Substack-style paid newsletters in spirituality/yoga sit at **$5–15/mo**, with **2–4% free→paid conversion** typical and **3–5% on engaged segments**. Trigger for conversion is consistently *community + voice trust*, not gated content volume.
- Local Ubud unlimited monthly passes (Yoga Barn $178, Radiantly Alive ~$114) define the **upper anchor** any Ubudian "premium" tier must sit *below* — we are not a studio, we curate access.

### Insight Timer paid (MemberPlus)

**Annual:** US$59.99 (~$5/mo effective). **Monthly:** $9.99/mo. ([insighttimer help](https://help.insighttimer.com/support/solutions/articles/67000664579-how-much-does-insight-timer-cost-), [insighttimer.com/member-plus](https://insighttimer.com/member-plus))

**What's free:** 100,000+ guided meditations, base timer, library browse.
**What's gated:** full course library (10/30-day programs), premium tracks, offline downloads, fast-forward/rewind, premium ambient timer.

Pattern: massive free library is the funnel; gated content is *depth* (courses) and *convenience* (offline, scrubbing), not breadth.

### Spiritual / conscious-community subscriptions

**Sounds True One:** $19.99/mo with 1-month free trial. Includes daily live classes (yoga, meditation, qigong), live evening sessions, members-only specials, and 20% discount on 2,000+ courses/books. ([soundstrue.com](https://www.soundstrue.com/products/sounds-true-one))

**Calm:** $16.99/mo or $79.99/yr (~$6.67/mo). Family plan $99.99/yr for 6 accounts. Lifetime $499.99. ([calm.com](https://www.calm.com/freetrial/plans))

**On Being (Krista Tippett):** No paid tier — donation-supported nonprofit; "The Pause" is a free monthly newsletter. *Unverified that they have any paid membership.* ([onbeing.org](https://onbeing.org/))

Conversion intuition from the category: **live + community + discount-on-extras** (Sounds True) commands a 3–4× premium over **content-only library** (Insight Timer, Calm annual). The price jump from $5 to $20 is gated by *liveness* and *people*, not by content volume.

### Newsletter & creator subscriptions

**Substack typical range:** $5–20/mo, with $5–15 the dense middle. ([reallygoodbusinessideas.com](https://www.reallygoodbusinessideas.com/p/substack-pricing))

**Conversion benchmarks:** 2–4% free→paid typical, 3–5% on engaged segments, up to 5–20% on small (<1k) hyper-engaged lists. Conversions build over 3–6 months as trust accrues. ([beehiiv blog](https://www.beehiiv.com/blog/how-much-money-can-you-make-from-1-000-newsletter-subscribers-in-2025))

**Conversion trigger pattern (consistent across sources):** "Community beats almost everything else… readers who deeply trust and value your voice are more likely to pay, especially for personal essays, commentary, or lived expertise." Gating *content* converts worse than gating *connection* (subscriber-only threads, AMAs, member events).

## Pricing recommendation for The Ubudian

**Free** — full event listings, story reading, weekly newsletter (current product). Loss-leader / SEO / discovery.

**Insider — $7/mo or $60/yr (~$5/mo annual)** — mirrors Insight Timer's $5/mo annual exactly, sits at the lower end of Substack's $5–15 band. Gates: subscriber-only weekly digest commentary, early access to event submissions before they go public, member-only WhatsApp/Discord ring, "what's in the field this week" voice-note from editor. Reasoning: this price point is **invisible** to the conscious-community spender (who's already paying $178/mo for Yoga Barn) but high enough to filter for genuine commitment. Annual prepay convention is non-negotiable in this category.

**Inner Ring — $25/mo or $240/yr (~$20/mo annual)** — sits just above Sounds True One ($19.99/mo) and well below any Ubud studio unlimited. Gates: monthly in-person gathering (low-key supper, ceremony, salon), introductions/intros to teachers and venue holders, behind-the-scenes editorial input, "ring" directory of ~150 trusted humans. Reasoning: **the only sustainable premium gate in this market is liveness + people, not content** — Sounds True confirms this. The $25 price is high enough to fund the in-person ops but cheap relative to Yoga Barn unlimited; positions Inner Ring as *the connector*, not a competitor to studios.

**Conversion target:** 3% of free → Insider, 0.5% of free → Inner Ring (consistent with Beehiiv/Substack benchmarks for engaged spiritual-niche newsletters). At 2,000 free subs that's 60 Insider × $5 = $300/mo + 10 Inner Ring × $20 = $200/mo = $500/mo MRR within 6 months of launch.

**Trial mechanic:** copy ClassPass — first 14 days free for Insider, no card required. The trial-to-paid conversion is where the work happens.
