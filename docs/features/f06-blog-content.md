# F06: Foundational Blog Content (Collaborative)

**Phase:** 2 — Content Platform
**Depends on:** F05
**Blocks:** None (but should be done before outreach)

---

## What

Three foundational blog posts that explain what The Ubudian is. These are written collaboratively — I ask questions, you answer in your voice, I craft drafts, you edit.

## The Three Posts

### Post 1: "What is The Ubudian?"
**Purpose:** Origin story for anyone landing on the site.
**Key beats:**
- The problem: community is fragmented, events buried in WhatsApp, newcomers lost
- Your personal connection to Ubud
- The vision: one home base for stories, events, experiences
- Who it's for (expat community, nomads, visitors who want the real Ubud)
- The tone: personal, warm, not corporate

**Questions to discuss:**
- When did you arrive in Ubud? What was your first experience like?
- What moment made you think "someone should build this"?
- What do you love most about the expat community here?
- How would you describe The Ubudian to a friend in one sentence?

### Post 2: "Humans of Ubud: An Invitation"
**Purpose:** Share with potential story subjects when approaching them.
**Key beats:**
- What Humans of Ubud is (community storytelling project)
- Who we're looking for (real people, real journeys — not influencers)
- What being featured involves (coffee, conversation, photos)
- What participants get (community recognition, their story told with care)
- What it ISN'T (promotional platform, influencer content)
- Sample story (possibly your own) to show the format

**Questions to discuss:**
- Why do you think these stories matter?
- What would you want someone to feel after reading a Humans of Ubud story?
- Can we use your own story as the first example?

### Post 3: "The Ubud Expat Experience"
**Purpose:** Editorial perspective piece. Establishes The Ubudian as a voice.
**Key beats:**
- The nature of Ubud's expat community — the magic and the messiness
- The silos and bubbles (yoga crowd, tech nomads, artists, wellness seekers)
- The overwhelm for newcomers
- Why this community specifically (not because locals aren't amazing — because this is the specific need)
- The vision: bridging the bubbles

**Questions to discuss:**
- What are the main "bubbles" you see in Ubud's expat scene?
- What do newcomers most struggle with?
- What's the most surprising thing about living here that outsiders don't expect?

## Process

1. We discuss the questions above (you share your thoughts)
2. I write full drafts from your answers
3. You review and edit for authenticity
4. We publish through the admin (F05)

## Verification

- All 3 posts published and visible on `/blog`
- Content reads authentically (your voice, not AI voice)
- Each post has a clear CTA (newsletter signup or "read more")
