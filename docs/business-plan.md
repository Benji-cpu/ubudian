# The Ubudian — Business Plan

---

## Positioning

**The Ubudian** is the insider's guide to Ubud, Bali — a community media brand that tells the stories of the expat community, curates what's worth doing, and offers experiences you can't find anywhere else.

It's the friend who's been in Ubud for years and knows everything. Not corporate. Personal, warm, embedded in the community.

**Core audience:** Ubud's expat and digital nomad community (8,000+ residents), plus tourists seeking authentic experiences.

---

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Humans of Ubud — The Soul
Photo + narrative stories of the expat community. Transformation stories, reinventions, healers, creatives, connectors. Creates emotional resonance, drives shares beyond Ubud, bridges community silos. Inspired by Humans of New York / Humans of Bombay.

### 2. The Weekly Newsletter — The Heartbeat
A curated weekly digest people look forward to. Not a boring events list — a personality-driven narrative of community life. Includes: featured story, "The Weekly Flow" (curated event journey), birthdays, community bulletin, cultural moments, sponsor slot.

### 3. Events Directory — The Utility
Free, open, searchable calendar of Ubud events. Community-submitted with moderation. The practical reason people visit the site. SEO engine for organic traffic.

---

## Revenue Model

### Stream 1: Secret Tours (Core Revenue)
Insider experiences led by you (and a network of vetted guides). Not tourist tours — "a day with a local friend." Themes: wellness, creative, spiritual, foodie, full immersion. $55-95/person, groups of 4-6. WhatsApp booking.

**Why this is the money maker:**
- Highest margin (60-70%)
- Leverages unique local knowledge
- Can't be replicated by a platform
- Tours → newsletter subscribers and vice versa (flywheel)
- Guide network scales without you running every tour

**Year 1 projection:** $18,000-30,000

### Stream 2: Newsletter Sponsorships
One sponsor per newsletter — a local business featured as a natural recommendation. Yoga studios, retreat centers, restaurants, wellness practitioners.

**Pricing:** $200-500/week depending on subscriber count.
**Key principle:** One sponsor only. Scarcity = premium pricing.

**Year 1 projection:** $9,600-14,400

### Stream 3: Retreat & Experience Affiliates
Recommend retreats in the newsletter and on site. Tracking links. 10-15% commission on bookings. Low effort, variable income.

**Year 1 projection:** $2,400-6,000

### Stream 4: Featured Business Listings (Phase 2)
Once site has traffic: local businesses pay $50-150/month for featured placement.

**Year 1 projection:** $0-3,000

### Revenue Summary

| Stream | Year 1 Monthly | Year 1 Annual |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Secret Tours | $1,500-2,500 | $18,000-30,000 |
| Newsletter Sponsors | $800-1,200 | $9,600-14,400 |

| Featured Listings | $0-500 | $0-3,000 |
| **Total** | **$2,500-4,700** | **$30,000-53,400** |

**Year 2 (with guide network + grown audience): $70,000-120,000**

## What We Don't Do

- **No monthly membership** — Legal risk with private events, doesn't fit communal culture
- **No event ticketing** — Megatix/Eventbrite do this. We link out, zero liability
- **No private/secret event listings** — Platform stays clean. Insider knowledge shared on tours, not online
- **No mobile app** — Responsive website is sufficient
- **No all-of-Bali scope** — Ubud-first, always

## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
- Launch website with blog posts + events directory + newsletter signup
- Shoot first 5-10 Humans of Ubud stories
- Manually populate events from WhatsApp/Facebook
- Announce in local community channels
- Personal outreach for initial 50+ subscribers

### Phase 2: Traction (Months 2-3)
- Weekly newsletter rhythm
- 500-1,000 subscribers
- First sponsor deal
- Launch first 1-2 tour themes
- Community starts submitting events

### Phase 3: Growth (Months 3-6)
- 2,000+ subscribers
- Sponsor rate increases
- Guide network: 2-3 additional guides
- Multi-language tours begin
- Community contributors writing columns

### Phase 4: Authority (Month 6+)
- 5,000+ subscribers
- Humans of Ubud recognized as cultural project
- Sponsor waitlist
- Exploring: podcast, book, video series, expand to Canggu

## The Engagement Engine

What makes people actually open the newsletter and come back:

- **Humans of Ubud stories** — emotional, shareable, the anchor
- **Birthday features** — low effort, high engagement, people share
- **The Weekly Flow** — curated event narrative, not a list
- **New arrival intros** — community bridging for newcomers
- **Weekly community question** — responses featured next week
- **Cultural moments** — Balinese calendar context, ceremonies
- **Community needs board** — "looking for a dentist," "free yoga Saturday"

---

## Audience Strategy

**Instagram = discovery channel.** Post story clips. Every post drives to newsletter.
**Website = home base.** Full stories, events, blog. SEO engine.
**Newsletter = the relationship.** The inbox. Weekly. Personal. Where community forms and revenue lives.

Hierarchy: Instagram catches attention → Website provides depth → Newsletter holds the relationship.
